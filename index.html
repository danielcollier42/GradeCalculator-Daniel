To calculatorize thy grade thou must enter thy grades and the weight of said grades.

When calculatorizing thy grade, thou must only enter the numbers separated by a comma and a space (ex. "86, 74, 99, 84"). Thou must not enter the percentage symbols when inputting thine grade.
